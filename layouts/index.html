Tom Broughton

Building Resilient Value-Driven Organisations · UK: 07717 726 487 · tom.a.broughton@gmail.com

I'm a business and technology transformation consultant, driven by a desire to effect positive change in the world. Using tools and techniques developed during the course of a career working in the public sector and large tech industries, I offer consultation, facilitation and coaching, building resilience in organisations where people and passion combine with technology. I am independent, impartial and value-driven.

Services

Transformation definition, management and support: I work collaboratively across all functions in an organisation to identify and enable more cost-effective and sustainable approaches to delivery.

  • Critical friend review - embedded risk and assurance: advising and making recommendations for pro-active intervention and transformation opportunities.
  • Goal setting and empirical roadmapping - defining programmes of change based on value-driven objectives which have the scope to flex as new opportunities and threats emerge.
  • Innovation integration - assisting with the design of "safe to fail" experiments and the integration of successful prototypes into core products and services.
  • Skills gap analysis and capability support - using my experience of developing digital, data and technology solutions, I advise on the skills required at specific phases of delivery. I coach and mentor existing staff and support hiring managers with the recruitment of specialists.
  • Governance and evolving operating models - developing governance and operating models that enable and empower teams through timely and appropriate decision-making and action to manage change across the "how" elements of organisational success.
  • Technical analysis and solution evaluation - impartial review of technical proposals during commissioning and of technical specifications for delivery teams.
